Expand description

Models mutable range that can only be reordered.

  • SemiOutputRange is extension of ForwardRange.
  • SemiOutputRange models primitive mutable range that just supports reordering of its elements.
  • For supporting reordering of elements, swap_at function is required. It allows to swap 2 elements at given positions.
